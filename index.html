Who Are We?

Overview

Operation Dudula was an informal association of politically conscious individuals and community leaders most of whom are from Soweto in JHB. Operation Dudula was an issue based community forum that dealt with a plethora of issues that affected the community of Soweto. Founding members came from different political parties with constituencies - it must be mentioned that the founding members are recruited from across different political organizations.

With the help of ordinary community members, founding members resolved to launch the organization in June 2021. Operation Dudula was registered as a not for profit company and name it Operation Dudula as a brand name. As a principle, the leadership resolved to subscribe to the following principles.

What We Stand For?

Founding Principals

  • Operation Dudula shall not associate with any political parties.
  • Operation Dudula shall exclusively work with other civic movements/forums and support communities directly.
  • Operation Dudula's involvement shall contribute towards jobs, economic and skills development of townships.
